HOW TO PLAY

  • ▲ Drag your finger anywhere on the trail to steer.
  • ▲ Keyboard players can use LEFT/RIGHT or A/D.
  • ▲ Grab Tuna Cans and Sardines for points.
  • ▲ Dodge Rocks, Logs, Brambles, and Cucumbers!
  • ▲ Snatch Yarn Shields or Feather Boosts!
  • ▲ Catch a Springy Branch to rest; swipe left or right when you are ready to drop.
  • ▲ Logs and brambles hurt twice. The trail gets faster and busier as you fall.
  • ▲ Keep falling to discover rivers, caves, torii paths, seasons, and weather.
